Know the Problem

  • What?
  • Who?
  • When?
  • Where?
  • Why?
  • How?

What?

The problem that we identified is the inefficiency of searching books when you go to any library, specially this Campus' library.

Who?

Students that want to research are the most affected by this problem due to the lack of information given when they want to find a book in the library. But not only the students suffer from this, also some of the teachers.

When?

This problem has been happening since the library was built and it happens every time a student wants to research from a physical book borrowed from the library.

Where?

This problem currently occurs at the itesm GDL Campus' library.

Why?

Clear instructions aren't given to the students/teachers that want to find a book. There isn't a good system that can provide the students the exact location of the material they are looking for.

How?

When the students want to look for a book in the library, they are given a code that should lead them to the place where the book is, the problem is that it is very difficult to understand this code and sometimes the book isn't there when the computer says it is.


Academic Research

You might be wondering... Why did we choose this problem and not something else? Well, first of all we are students from second semester of college that studied high school in the same Campus. We've visited the library many times to look for a book that fulfilled our needs and every time we went there the problem was the same. It is very difficult to find the book! Even if you ask for help, it takes centuries for you to get your hands on the book. But we are not the only ones who have had this problem, most of our friends preffer to research on the internet since the process of renting a book from the library takes too much time, and in college you can't afford to lose it.


Social Impact

Students arent used to search for information in books because they consider it takes too much time and they are right, since the search of the book is way slower. Both students and teachers are being less eficient at their work and homeworks for the extra time it takes them to find a useful book. Most of the students prefer to look up information on the internet since it's faster and easier. The result of doing this is that most of the information found on the internet might not be accurate whatsoever.
